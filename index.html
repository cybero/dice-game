GAME OBJECTIVE


The first player to get a total score of 100 points wins!




-> Click 'ROLL DICE' as many times as you wish on your turn.



-> If you roll a '1', you will lose all of your current score and end your turn.



-> To build up your score, bank it by simply clicking on the 'HOLD' button.



-> Your current round score is shown in the 'CURRENT' red box.



-> Your total score is shown in red font under your 'PLAYER' name.
